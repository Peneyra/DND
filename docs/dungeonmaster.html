Dungeon Master Guide

Scenario Setup

The scenario begins at Ye Ol' Fiddle. The adventurer wakes up in a private booth in the tavern, with no recollection of why they are there. They are new to the town and do not know a soul.

Key NPC: Elias "Strings" Marrow, a fiddler, found the adventurer unconscious in the sailor’s quarters and brought them here.

The adventurer is hungry, preventing them from fully hearing conversations until they eat.

Rumors in the Tavern

  • A group near the door discusses outsiders who purchased a warehouse by the barge docks, fired locals, yet still show activity.
  • A group near the stage discusses bandits raiding an upstream barge, but the riverine coastguard repelled them.

Pantheon of Port Dunwyd

The city recognizes the Dawn War Pantheon, but the Arcanum suppresses open religious expression.

  • Asmodeus – God of Tyranny and Domination
  • Bahamut – Dragon god of Justice and Protection
  • Pelor – God of the Sun and Time
  • ...and many others.

Economy and Trade

The world operates on a standardized monetary system:

  • 10 copper pieces (cp) = 1 silver piece (sp)
  • 10 silver pieces (sp) = 1 gold piece (gp)
  • 10 gold pieces (gp) = 1 platinum piece (pp)

Types of Magic

Magic is divided into three categories:

  • Physical Magic: Enhances physical actions.
  • Arcane Magic: Draws from nature or emotion.
  • Pious Magic: Derived from divine favor.

Port Dunwyd Overview

Port Dunwyd is a coastal trade hub divided into districts:

  • Grand Harbor: Merchant's hub.
  • Sailor’s Quarter: Dockside entertainment.
  • University District: Center of knowledge and magic.
  • Barracks & Fortress: Military stronghold.
